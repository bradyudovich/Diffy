Accrual of Hilton Honors Milestone Bonus Points

If a Member achieves one hundred (100) qualifying nights within a calendar year, they will be able to gift Diamond Elite Tier Status to a Member of their choice. Gifted Elite Tier Status will begin immediately upon approval of the gift and last 365 days from the date approved. After 365 days, the gifted Member's Elite Tier Status will change to the Elite Tier Status that they would have earned without the gifted status in the previous calendar year.
Members meet gifting qualifications the following year can gift status to the same Member that was previously gifted Elite Tier Status. Members will have to re-elect the Member receiving gifted Elite Tier Status each year.  Elite Tier Status gifts do not automatically carry over if unused.
Elite Tier Status gifted to another Member cannot be changed during the same year.
The Member receiving the gifted status must be enrolled as a Hilton Honors Member.  Elite Tier Status cannot be gifted to someone who does not have a Hilton Honors account.

Reward stays
(including Points & Money Rewards™ stays) count as full qualifying nights and are counted towards achieving gifting status.
